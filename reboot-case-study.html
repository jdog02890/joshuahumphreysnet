The problem

Reboot Computers of Jax had outgrown their old website. The previous design was built on a dated template with a generic blue and white color scheme, cluttered layouts, and pages that didn't do justice to the quality of their work. Key services like custom PC builds and business solutions were buried in crowded pages, and the overall look didn't match the reputation they'd built with thousands of customers and over 500 businesses served.

They needed a site that looked as good as the PCs they build.

What I did

I rebuilt the entire site from scratch. New visual identity with a dark, modern color scheme that matches the tech-forward nature of the business. Every page was reorganized with clearer hierarchy, stronger calls to action, and a layout that guides visitors toward contacting the shop or browsing inventory.

Key changes included:

A completely new dark theme with the existing green and blue brand colors working against a dark background instead of white. A redesigned homepage with a rotating hero, trust badges, and clear service cards. Rebuilt service pages (Custom Builds, Repairs, E-Recycling, Business Solutions) with better structure and pricing visibility. Improved mobile responsiveness across every page. Cleaner navigation with a sticky header, contact button, and phone number always visible. Better footer with organized links, address, and warranty information.
